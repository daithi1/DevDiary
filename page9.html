Ninth log: 04/04/26

At this point we basically had an alpha version of our game completed. The next thing I wanted to add was some sort of spawner for the zombies. The idea would be when you enter a room, the zombies would spawn in random locations in the room. This ended up not being as hard as I thought it was, although maybe I set my expectations really high after the shock of creating a zombie AI hit me. Going back to my test project I basically set up a trigger. You can move this trigger around quite easily, so when adding it to the game, all you have to do is simply move it to where the door is or wherever you want it so that when you pass through that area, the zombies spawn. On top of this, I link a radius to it that you can edit and adjust to whatever size you want. This radius gives you the area where the zombies can spawn upon entering the trigger. I was also able to make it so that the zombies can't spawn in places like on top of a pillar or any object in the room, in case they get stuck on spawn.
