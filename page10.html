Final Entry!!!

Final log: 15/04/26

Today was our final meeting yay!!! It is also a bit sad to be honest. We had spent so much time and effort working together to make our first game which surprisingly wasn't a complete disaster! I hope the others can agree but I had quite an enjoyable time working on this project. Yeah there were quite a lot of roadblocks but eventually we did get over them and delivered a pretty good final product. I definitely learned a lot about the benefits AND especially the struggles of working in a team. But I think that is and experience that everyone must go through because it will be a very handy skill to have for future careers. Unfortunately in the finished product I didn't get my zombie spawner fully working so we never added it, to avoid any malfunctions. But I am definitely still so proud of how far we came.
Final Words:

Thank you to my team for being so great to work with and getting through all of this respectfully. It has been a pleasure getting to work on this with them. One thing I forgot to mention was one of our members joined quite late, as he was unable to come to Ireland due to an injury, for quite a long amount of time. Yet he stilled joined our calls to see what we were getting up to and helping in any way he could. I do feel like I could have done a better job making this game as there were some moments where me, along with a lot of us procrastinated quite a bit with our tasks. But eventually we all pulled through so that's what really matters.
